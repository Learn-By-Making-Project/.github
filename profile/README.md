# Welcome to Learn-By-Making-Projects

**Learn-By-Making-Projects** is a collaborative hub where anyone can start, build, and share interesting projects. The goal is simple: learn by doing, collaborate with others, and grow together.

## 🚀 What You Can Do

* Start new projects
* Collaborate with other members
* Share your ideas and progress
* Fork and build upon existing work
* Learn through hands-on experience

## 📂 Repository Types

### Public Repositories

Open to everyone!

* Explore freely
* Collaborate openly
* Fork and build upon ideas

### Private Repositories

Members can create private projects for focused collaboration.

* Private repositories **cannot be forked** unless the owner grants explicit permission.

## 🤝 How to Join & Collaborate

* Request to join or propose a project
* Use **GitHub Issues** and **Discussions** to communicate
* Ask questions, suggest ideas, and contribute
* Follow our [Code of Conduct](./CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)

## 📏 Community Guidelines

1. Respect other members and their work
2. Always ask permission before forking private repositories
3. Keep collaboration positive and constructive
4. Learn together — don’t hesitate to ask for help
5. Share knowledge and support others
